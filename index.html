WATER POLLUTION NEEDS A FAST SOLUTION.

Water is an essential resource for all life on Earth.


Water is a precious substance present abundantly on Earth. The main body of water is oceans which cover 96.5% of the Earth's surface and is the home for maximum living organisms. Water serves as a vital resource to keep life going.


When sediment and other pollutants enter the water, they smother coral reefs, speed the growth of damaging algae, and lower water quality.
